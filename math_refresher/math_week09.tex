\section{Week 9 Refresher: Retrieval Metrics and Evaluation Arithmetic}

\subsection*{Setting the Scene}
This week is where model math meets system math. Retrieval and evaluation require clear metric definitions; otherwise, comparisons drift into opinion.

\subsection*{Core Reminders}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.5em]
  \item \textbf{Precision/recall at $k$:} always define denominator conventions.
  \item \textbf{Lexical vs dense scoring:} BM25 and cosine answer different matching questions.
  \item \textbf{Fusion logic:} rank fusion can improve robustness when retrieval signals disagree.
  \item \textbf{Evaluation decomposition:} separate retrieval quality from generation quality.
\end{itemize}
