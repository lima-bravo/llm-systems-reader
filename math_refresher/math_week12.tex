\section{Week 12 Refresher: Diagnostic Decomposition and Multi-Objective Reasoning}

\subsection*{Setting the Scene}
The closing week asks for disciplined diagnosis: separate failure sources before choosing interventions. The mathematics is modest, but clarity in decomposition is essential.

\subsection*{Core Reminders}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.5em]
  \item \textbf{Axis decomposition:} structure, meaning, context, grounding, environment are different diagnostic questions.
  \item \textbf{Conditional analysis:} inspect error rates by slice before global averages.
  \item \textbf{Correlation vs intervention:} fit quality does not imply control leverage.
  \item \textbf{Tradeoff surfaces:} optimize over accuracy, safety, latency, and cost jointly.
\end{itemize}
