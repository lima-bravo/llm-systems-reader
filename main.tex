\section*{Preface}
\addcontentsline{toc}{section}{Preface}

This reader is intended for readers with a strong background in mathematics,
statistics, and computer science who wish to reason rigorously about modern
Large Language Model (LLM) systems.

The focus is not on using LLMs as black boxes, but on understanding them as
\emph{engineered socio-technical systems}, integrating:
\begin{itemize}
  \item probabilistic modeling,
  \item large-scale optimization,
  \item systems engineering tradeoffs,
  \item human factors and failure modes.
\end{itemize}

Each weekly handout combines theory, systems design considerations, and
practical experimentation.

\subsection*{Reading Order and Chapter Dependencies}
\addcontentsline{toc}{subsection}{Reading Order and Chapter Dependencies}

The chapters are arranged so that each one's notation and results build on
its predecessors. Figure~\ref{fig:chapter_dependencies} shows the directed
dependency graph; arrows point from a source chapter to any chapter that
